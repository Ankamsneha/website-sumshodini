Welcome to INNOTREK

Round 1: QR UNVEIL

"QR Unveil"! This round is of decoding patterns and code snippet within the QR codes. At various stations, participants will encounter different QR codes, each presenting a unique challenge to decode. The patterns concealed within these codes will test your ability to discern and understand the hidden sequence or logic. As an elimination round, participants must strive to successfully decode the QR codes to progress further in the competition.

Round 2: WHISPER WAVE

"Dive into the fun of 'Whisper Wave'! "A game where a team passes messages down the line, but with a twist! One person starts with a secret sentence of technical words, whispers it to the next, and the message cascades through the team. The catch? Everyone else has headphones on with music, making communication hilariously unpredictable!"

Round 3: MYSTIC MIND HIVE

Level 1: 'Balloon Blitz' demands swift balloon bursts to unveil the first numerical clue. Level 2: 'Enigma Enclave,' solve intricate puzzles to decode the second crucial digit. Level 3: 'Visual Voyage,' requires eagle-eyed observation to uncover the hidden number within vivid images. Collect all three numbers to unlock the ultimate treasure chest and claim victory!"
