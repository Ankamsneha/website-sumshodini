Welcome to LOGIC LORDS 2.O

Round 1: MIND BENDER

A crossword puzzle which is to be solved with the clues provided based on programming.

Round 2: PICTIONARY

It is a guessing game in which the participants have to look at the picture and guess related to the picture whoever is the first one to guess the maximum number of pictures wins the round.

Round 3: THE THRILL OF THE HUNT

In this round the participants are provided with different kinds of jigsaw puzzles the participants who finish the puzzle first will get the clues simultaneously other participants also get the clues to find the key to unlock the room. Top performers will be awarded. Everyone will be provided with a participation certificate.
