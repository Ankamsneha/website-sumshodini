Round 1: CHITCODE

In this round, there are two jars. One contains small coding questions related to the C language, while the other holds the corresponding answers. Participants randomly select a question chit from the first jar, solve it within a specified time, and then check the answer from the second jar. This interactive format adds an element of randomness and time pressure to the coding challenge, making it both engaging and dynamic.

Round 2: EXECU-PUZZLE

Dive into the corporate world with our Cross Words challenge. This round Unravels MNC-related terms to unlock the puzzle showcasing your knowledge.

Round 3: BID-IQ

Participants bid for the chance to answer all questions from 1 to 10. The event manager initiates the first bid, setting the tone for subsequent rounds. The bidding process is a crucial aspect, introducing a competitive edge and strategy to secure the right to answer each question. There are 10 questions in total. For questions 1-7, everyone can participate. Starting from question 8, only participants with the highest points are eligible to answer. For question 9, the top 3 scorers get the chance, and for question 10, only the participants with the top 2 scorers among them can answer. This setup ensures an inclusive start and gradually narrows down to the top-performing participants as the quiz progresses.
