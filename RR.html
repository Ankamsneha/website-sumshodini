Welcome to RIDDLES AND ROUTES

Round 1: HOUSIE HUNT

In this unique version of BINGO, organizers take the stage to ask 25 questions to the participants which consists of basic programming questions, brand slogans, full forms with clues. For each correct answer, participant gets a chance to tell a number that is to be striken by all others. Top answered participants and quick BINGO announcers are qualified for next round.

Round 2: SCOUT TRAIL

In this round participants need to dive into a box filled with various electronic components. Participants are provided with a paper that consists of riddles or clues for identifying the electronic components. After decoding the clue, participants need to identify the respective component from the box.

Round 3: SCAVENGERS HUNT

Top 8 participants from both the rounds are eligible for this round. A list of clues that reveals a certain thing are given on a paper. That is to be decoded and discovered in and around the college. First Charm to find all the things and reach the destination wins a surprise gift.
