Welcome to DIVE INTO FUN

Round 1

Participants should take selfies with different brand logos as many as possible in given time limit. Scores will be based on no of logos they collected. according to scores participants will be qualified to next round

Round 2

Technical quiz, some technical questions will be asked and which participant first answers correctly he/she will get the point based on the scores participants will be qualified to next round

Round 3

some group of participants will be given each character in an appocalyptic situation(ex: plane crash etc), so they should discuss among each other and choose one to survive and explain with reasons based on their discussions and explanations faculty co-ordinators will give scores.
