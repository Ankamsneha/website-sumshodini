Welcome to TECH MATRIX

Round 1: CODE QUEST CARNIVAL

The participants are given some of the Highlighting the Syntax, Algorithms and Output Logic. So, the participants should guess the Output, Errors and Title of the respective program.

Round 2: JUMBLE GENIUS HUNT

This Round completely deals with Technical Knowledge. The Participants are provided with Jumbled Words with a clue, Guessing the word by providing length with a clue etc.

Round 3: CIRCUIT CHRONICLE

The participants are provided with some un-labelled components to design the given circuit practically. The participants who complete earlier are declared as Winners.
