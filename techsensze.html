Welcome to TECH SENZE

Round 1

Participants are shown a frame of 8 to 10 images at one go for 5-10sec. They have to memorize them and should tell whatever images they remember. Likewise 4 to 5 frames are shown There is no elimination in this round.

Round 2

Participants need to play this as a team of 2. A box of 16 words is shown and those words are related to eachother (for eg. 4flowers, 4vehicles, 4gadgets, 4colors, total 16) and they need to identify the connection of 4groups.Similarly 4 to 5 boxes will be displayed. Teams who identifies the connection are qualified for the round 3.

Round 3

A circuit is shown and the participants should make the circuit in software. It will be done in simulation method. Whoever makes it first and shows the output is considered as 1st winner. 1 st and 2nd winners will be awarded with exciting cash prizes and all the participants will get participation certificates.
