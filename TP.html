TECH TOPIA!!

Welcome to TECH TOPIA

Round 1: LOGOQUEST

In this round, participants will be presented with various logos associated with brands and tech companies. They have a 5 second window to provide quick and accurate answers, and the fastest and most accurate responses will advance to the next round

Round 2: TURBO TRIVIA

In this round, teams go head-to-head in a fast trivia face-off featuring general knowledge and technical questions. Quick thinking is essential because every incorrect answer or missed response results in the team holding hefty books. The ultimate winner is determined either by accumulating correct answers or till the opposing team drop their weights.

Round 3: DART-POP

In this ultimate round, participants will engage in bursting balloons using darts. Inside each balloon, questions pertaining to logical reasoning, aptitude, basic C questions and more are concealed. Participants must respond to these questions upon successfully bursting the balloons.
